Our Story

Attmia Honey was born from a simple love for nature and a promise to bring you the purest honey possible.

We work directly in the wildflower fields of Andhra Pradesh. Every jar comes from bees that forage freely — no sugar feeding, no antibiotics, no heat processing.

Attmia means "Dear One". We treat every bee and every customer like family.
