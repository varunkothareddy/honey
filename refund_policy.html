Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 13, 2025

Thank you for choosing Attmia Natural Honey. As we deal in perishable food products, our return and refund policy is designed to ensure hygiene, safety, and quality while complying with Indian food regulations.

General Policy

Honey is a consumable food item, therefore we have a strict no-return and no-refund policy for reasons such as change of mind, natural crystallization (which is normal and does not affect quality), or personal preference.

Exceptions (Eligible for Return/Replacement)

  • Damaged packaging or leaked/sealed-broken product upon delivery.
  • Wrong product delivered (different from your order).
  • Expired or adulterated product (though we guarantee freshness).

In these rare cases, we will offer a replacement or full refund at our discretion.

How to Request a Return/Replacement

  • Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery via WhatsApp (+91 93456 56553) or email (attmianaturalproducts@gmail.com).
  • Provide clear photos/videos of the damaged/wrong product and packaging.
  • Do not consume or open the product further.

Replacement products will be delivered within 7 days

Process Timeline

  • We will verify your claim within 48 hours.
  • If approved, replacement will be shipped free of cost, or refund processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method/bank account.
  • Return shipping (if required) is paid by us only if the error is ours.

Cancellations

Orders cannot be cancelled once confirmed via phone call and processing has begun. Please review your order carefully before confirmation.
