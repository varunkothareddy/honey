Shipping & Delivery Policy

We deliver pure, natural honey with love — all across India.

Delivery Timeline

  • Orders are processed within 24–48 hours of placement.
  • Delivery takes 3 to 7 working days depending on your location.
  • Metro cities & major towns: 3–5 days
  • Remote & rural areas: 5–7 days
  • You will receive a confirmation call from our team within 24 hours to verify your order and address.

Packaging

Each bottle is carefully packed in custom-fitted, eco-friendly boxes with protective cushioning to ensure your honey arrives safe and intact — just as pure as it left our farms.

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, you will receive a call from our delivery partner. We currently do not provide online tracking numbers, but our team stays in direct touch with you until delivery.

Important Notes

  • We do not accept cancellations once the order is confirmed via phone call.
  • We do not offer returns or refunds as this is a food product.
  • In case of non-delivery due to incorrect address or unavailability, the order will be returned to us and re-delivery charges may apply.
